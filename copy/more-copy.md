# Supporting Copy

## About Us
River City Martial Arts Academy was created to give Richmond-area families a structured, positive place to train. Our classes focus on technique, confidence, discipline, and steady progress, with programs designed to meet students where they are.

## Facility
Our studio includes a clean padded training floor, viewing and waiting space for families, simple equipment storage, and a welcoming front desk area. The environment is organized, safe, and beginner-friendly.

## Parking
Parking is available in the lot beside the building and along nearby side streets. Please arrive a few minutes early for your first visit.

## Intro Offer
New students can request a free intro class to tour the studio, meet an instructor, and try a beginner-friendly session.

## What to Bring
Wear comfortable athletic clothing and bring a water bottle. Uniform details can be discussed after the intro class.
